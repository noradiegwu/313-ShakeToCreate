Compare this to classic cocktails:

Daiquiri

Daiquiri is a family of drinks that first became popular in the early 1900s. They generally consist of rum, lime juice, and a sweetener.

Manhattan

The Manhattan is a cocktail first popularized in New York clubs in early 1870s. It traditionally is a stirred cocktail built with whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters.

Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned is a simple cocktail made by muddling sugar with alcohol and garnishing with a twist of expressed citrus peel.

Whiskey Sour

The Whiskey Sour is made with whiskey, lemon juice, sweetener, and traditionally shaken with an egg white.

Martini

The Martini is a classic cocktail that is made with gin and dry vermouth garnished with an olive or lemon.

Fitzgerald

The Fitzgerald is a variation on the Gin Sour that adds Angostura bitters. It's made with gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, and bitters.
